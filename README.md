# Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 💼 Business Impact
- Identified top-performing regions driving the majority of revenue  
- Revealed seasonal sales patterns for demand forecasting  
- Highlighted opportunities to optimize sales distribution across regions  

---

## 📊 Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes regional sales performance, focusing on revenue distribution, sales trends, and country-level insights.

The goal is to support data-driven decision-making by identifying key growth regions, seasonal demand patterns, and overall sales efficiency.

## 📈 Dashboard Analysis

### Regional Sales Distribution
- Identifies dominant markets by revenue contribution  
- United States contributes ~70% of total sales  

### Monthly Sales Trend
- Detects seasonality patterns  
- Peak performance observed in March and May  

### Sales by Country
- Compares performance across multiple regions  
- Highlights geographic revenue concentration  

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Sales are heavily concentrated in a single region (United States)  
- Strong seasonal peaks indicate demand cycles  
- Potential opportunity to diversify revenue across other regions
